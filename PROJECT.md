# Fox Travel Egypt Website

## Project Overview

**Fox Travel Egypt** is a commercial travel booking website for a real travel company based in Hurghada, Egypt. The website is **conversion-focused**, not a blog or brochure.

### One-Line Summary
Fox Travel Egypt is a booking-focused travel website that allows tourists in Hurghada to discover, book, and pay for trips, sea excursions, and private transfers through a modern, mobile-first experience.

---

## Purpose

The main goal of the website is to allow tourists to:
- **Discover** trips and transfers
- **Book** trips and transfers easily
- **Pay** for bookings online securely
- **Contact** the company via WhatsApp

---

## What The Website Offers

### 1. Trips & Tours
- **Sea trips**: Snorkeling, diving, islands, yacht trips
- **Desert safari trips**
- **City tours**: Cairo, Luxor, and other destinations
- **Day trips and multi-day tours**

### 2. Transfers & Transportation
- **Airport transfers**: Hurghada Airport ↔ Hotels
- **City-to-city transfers**
- **Private transportation**

### 3. Accommodation (Secondary Feature)
- Hotels and resorts in Hurghada
- Optional travel packages (hotel + trip + transfer)

---

## Target Users

- **Tourists visiting Hurghada**
- **International travelers** from Europe, UK, Russia, Middle East
- **Mostly mobile users**
- Users who want **quick prices** and **simple booking**
- Heavy **WhatsApp usage** for communication

---

## User Goals

Users visit the website to:
- Find the best trips in Hurghada
- Book sea activities and excursions
- Arrange airport or hotel transfers
- Pay online securely or contact via WhatsApp
- Trust the company and feel safe booking

---

## Business Goals

- **Increase online bookings**
- **Reduce manual WhatsApp work**
- **Build trust and professionalism**
- **Show clear pricing**
- **Support future growth** (more trips, languages, locations)

---

## Key Features

- **Trips listing & trip details pages**

---

## Important Guidelines for Development

### Core Principle
You are building a **real production website** for a travel company in Egypt.

### Conversion Focus
Every page should help users either:
- ✅ **Book a trip**
- ✅ **Book a transfer**
- ✅ **Contact the company**
- ✅ **Build trust** to convert later

### Design Priorities
- Mobile-first approach
- Clear, visible pricing
- Simple booking flows
- Trust-building elements
- Fast loading times
- Easy WhatsApp contact
